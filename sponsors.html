Our Sponsors
RM Robotics owes a great thanks to all of its supporters, who we have listed below. If you are interested in supporting RM Robotics and STEM education in Montgomery County, please consider joining them.
Rockville Rotary Club
The Rockville Rotary Club is one of RM Robotics' oldest sponsors, having supported us since 2011, our second season. The Rotary Club is a group of Rockvillians dedicated to providing assistance to service projects throughout Rockville as part of the Rotary Club's mission of "Service above Self." The Rockville chapter holds an annual Twilight Runfest every July in Rockville Town Center.
JRC
JRC Integrated Systems, Inc offers a variety of technology solutions to the US Navy, US Department of Defense, and other US Government agencies. JRC has been a sponsor since 2011.
Alliance Material Handling
Alliance Material Handling specializes in loading equipment and other varieties of industrial machinery. They operate four locations throughout the Midatlantic region. Alliance Material Handling was a Championship 2014 Sponsor.
Carey
Carey operates a fleet of chaufferred vehicles available for any occassion. Carey is a worldwide leader in quality, reliable transportation, and graciously helped Team 5421 attend last year's FTC World Championship in St. Louis, Missouri. Carey was a Championship 2014 Sponsor.
Piggybackr
Thank you to everyone who donated to our Piggybackr page to help us get to the World Championship in 2014. The trip would absolutely not have been possible without your overwhelming support. United Therapeutics
United Therapeutics Corporation is a biotechnology company focused on the development and commercialization of unique products to address the unmet medical needs of patients with chronic and life-threatening conditions. In pursuit of "Medicines for life"(R), UniTher's staff research treatments for pulmonary arterial hypertenstion, cancer, and some of the world's most complicated viral illnesses. United Therapeutics have been a sponsor since 2015.
Eastland Foods
Eastland Foods specializes in importing premium Asian food products into the United States. Eastland VP Ed Mekhaya volunteers his time, engineering expertise, and life experience to help improve robots and team members alike. Eastland has been a sponsor since 2012.
Richard Montgomery High School PTSA
The Richard Montgomery PTSA (Parent, Teacher, Student Association) aims to connect the entire RM community through workshops, events, and fundraising. The RMHS PTSA has been a sponsor since 2012.
Capital Bank
Capital Bank is dedicated to providing the friendliest, most responsive banking services to buisnesses, professionals, not-for-profit organizations and investors in the Washington DC metropolitan area. Capital Bank has been a sponsor since 2014.
STEMaction
STEMaction, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit that delivers exciting science, technology, engineering and math (STEM) activities to K-12 students and builds a local community of support for the programs and participants. STEMaction is by, for and about the local community in Maryland. STEMaction was a Super Regionals 2014 sponsor.
FJB Engineering
FJB Engineering is a Rockville-area mechanical engineering and machining leader, specializing in designing and manufacturing high-precision parts for aerospace and electronics applications. Founding Partner Frank Bloom has provided RM Robotics with useful supplies as well as donated machine time to help produce a practice field for the past season.
Troodon Software
Troodon Software offers software and systems development services for a wide variety of platforms and devices. Troodon's founder, Frank Hunleth, shares his software expertise with RM Robotics and has taught a series of classes about collaborative software development for team members.
Renaissance Arts Center
Renaissance Arts offers a variety of structured progressive, term-based courses for students of all ages as well as specialized camps and intensive workshops throughout the year. Renaissance Arts Center has been a sponsor since 2014.
Sparkfun Electronics
Sparkfun Electronics is a revolutionary electronics website with parts for just about any project. At the forefront of the Open Source and Maker movements, Sparkfun also provides a vast wealth of electronics tutorials- which have proved of great use to team members.
