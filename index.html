RM Robotics

We are a club from Rockville, MD, that fields two teams in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). RM Robotics has been competing in FTC since 2010.

Our name, RM Robotics, is short for Rockville-Montgomery Robotics, an homage to our amazing community.
