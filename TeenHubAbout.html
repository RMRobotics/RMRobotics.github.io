About Us

TeenHub was founded last year by the Rockville-Montgomery Robotics Association, Inc., an IRS-recognized non-profit organization that fields FTC Team 5421. Interested in increasing programming literacy in their local community, TeenHub focuses on teaching Python and HTML basics in a monthly/bi-monthly workshop-style at local libraries.
