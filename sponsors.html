Our Sponsors
RM Robotics owes a great thanks to all of its supporters, who we have listed below. If you are interested in supporting RM Robotics and STEM education in Montgomery County, please consider joining them.
Rockville Rotary Club
The Rockville Rotary Club is one of RM Robotics' oldest sponsors, having supported us since our second season. The Rotary Club is a group of Rockvillians dedicated to providing assistance to service projects throughout Rockville as part of the Rotary Club's mission of "Service above Self." The Rockville chapter holds an annual Twilight Runfest every July in Rockville Town Center.
JRC
JRC Integrated Systems, Inc offers a variety of technology solutions to the US Navy, US Department of Defense, and other US Government agencies.
Troodon Software
Troodon Software offers software and systems development services for a wide variety of platforms and devices. Troodon's founder, Frank Hunleth, shares his software expertise with RM Robotics and has taught a series of classes about collaborative software development for team members.
Eastland Foods
Eastland Foods specializes in importing premium Asian food products into the United States. Eastland VP Ed Mekhaya volunteers his time, engineering expertise, and life experience to help improve robots and team members alike.
Aristotle Circle
Aristotle Circle provides a variety of tutoring and educational consulting services to high school students. They are also always looking to hire successful students, and have offered employment to several team members through the Aristotle Peers program.
Bluecotton
Bluecotton is an online leader in custom t-shirts, apparel, and promotional items. They combine great pricing with even better service, and we highly recommend them to any team looking to purchase team shirts or other items.
FJB Engineering
FJB Engineering is a Rockville-area mechanical engineering and machining leader, specializing in designing and manufacturing high-precision parts for aerospace and electronics applications. Founding Partner Frank Bloom has provided RM Robotics with useful supplies as well as donated machine time to help produce a practice field for the past season.
Carey
Carey operates a fleet of chaufferred vehicles available for any occassion. Carey is a worldwide leader in quality, reliable transportation, and graciously helped Team 5421 attend last year's FTC World Championship in St. Louis, Missouri.
Alliance Material Handling
Alliance Material Handling specializes in loading equipment and other varieties of industrial machinery. They operate four locations throughout the Midatlantic region.
Sparkfun Electronics
Sparkfun Electronics is a revolutionary electronics website with parts for just about any project. At the forefront of the Open Source and Maker movements, Sparkfun also provides a vast wealth of electronics tutorials- which have proved of great use to team members.
