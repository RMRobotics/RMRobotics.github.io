RM Robotics

We are a non-profit organization originating in Rockville, MD, that fields a team in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). RM Robotics has been competing in FTC since 2010.

Our name, RM Robotics, is short for Rockville-Montgomery Robotics, paying homage to our amazing community.
